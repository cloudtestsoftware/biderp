Believe in quality of product
BidERP is an integrated cloud platform for bidding, contracting and construction management for any size of construction project. Our simplified platform can save your time and money by solving critical business challenges and you can focus your energy for faster cost effective project delivery by engaging contractor and sub contractor in single platform. You can gather project critical data and take quicker decision to avoid project delivery issues . and save cost due to delay. We provide complete ERP solutions in cloud with mode than 20 modules for Bid and Construction management. Below are few sample modules in BidERP.

Step-1: Setup project template, site, phases and part list

Create reusable project template data, create project site and identify different phases of the project, create part lists from different suppliers and vendors.

Step-2 :Prepare initial quote and identify lists of items

Prepare initial Quote for each project for your site as rate basis, MMN or both. Prepare list of material and resources you need to procure. Compare cost and profitability against initial estimation.

Step-3: Start Bidding process

After you made initial project estimation and prepared list of jobs need to be completed with Bill of materials and resources, ready with project specifications and bidding documents, you can start the bidding process for your project and send invitation to list of vendors. Once you receive the Bid from list of qualified vendors, you can evaluate bids and select Qualified bidder and make a contract.

Step-4 : Prepare and Submit Bid

After bidders are notified, they can accept or reject the invitation. On acceptance of invitation , they can prepare required Bid proposal, cost of items , plan and other supporting documents, reply Bid Quiz sent by the owner. If your Bid is accepted selected bidder will be notified and a contract will be signed.

Step-5 : Create project contract , monitor progress & costs

Once the contract is signed, Owner can create a project and evaluate each jobs, activities, cost and progress. Owner can assign budget and evaluate actual cost, estimation , change order cost and profitability in real time. Monitor purchase order , material delivery, scheduling and many more..

Step-6: Final estimation , cost differences and profitability

Real time data against planned resource vs. actual cost, change order cost , resource supplement , cost of delay , budget deficiency are critical parameters to measure the overall health of the project. Take necessary action to adjust the project and its time line to achieve desired result.

Step-7 : Project scheduling & planning

Project planning and scheduling are very important functions to complete project within time line. Real time monitoring of each plan, it's progress, risk and identifying required adjustment in ahead of time is very important to hit any milestone. Identifying the impact of any delay to any critical milestone to the overall project schedule is very important to achieve the target delivery date.

Step-8: Monitor budget and financial planning

For any project to be successful, it is very important to plan and measure the allocation of budget, , overall financial planning against real time project progress, delivery time line vs. budget burned out, cash flow and pending cost of invoices, delay in financial transactions and delivery of critical milestones, financial projection and planning are very important parameters to look at and take preventive action in ahead of time.

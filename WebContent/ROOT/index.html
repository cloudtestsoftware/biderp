In many cases, in construction it is difficult to establish linkage between cost of actual job with its initial and final estimation in multiple level of contract and it's changes. Sometimes it becomes an issue where the cost is because of new changes or was part of the initial contract. If it is covered within initial scope of work what is the difference of cost because of any new change after the initial scope. Also it may necessary to measure the percentage of budget which already spent and compare to the cost estimated. Whether the projected cost is much higher than initial budget and how soon we can know about the latest change of budget that is needed to avoid further delay of the project.

BidERP provides a direct linkage between your different level of estimations within and outside of the scope of work and also identify in which level of contract the change cost is coming higher than the estimation. BidERP also supports changes in multiple stages of the project, add new changes, measure change cost separately compare to initial scope, provides linkage between cost of initial contract and sub contract, generates alerts based on the budget setting, resolves timely payment issues and avoids delay of work.

BidERP also supports a single platform for budgeting and accounting of the project cost, provides direct linkage of funds and expenses, measures funds available to spend vs. burned out, generates alerts to take preventive measure in ahead of time such that unnecessary delay of project can be avoided due to the flow of funds.
